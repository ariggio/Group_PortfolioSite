About Me

I'm an artist and web designer and developer based in New York.

I decided to study Web App and Development at Hunter Business School in order to add to my skill set. In 2014 I graduated Marywood University with a BFA in Illustration, and ever since I've been a Freelance Illustrator and a Paint and Sip Instructor. I've always had an interests in web desgin and programming (and programming in general), so this was the perfect opportunity for me to finally start learning.

Out of everything that I've learned so far, I feel most versed with front-end web design. I would love to get into the overall designing aspect of Web App so that I may take full advantage of my background in Illustration.
